About Songa Capital Ltd

Songa Capital Ltd is a leading financial services provider across Africa, dedicated to fighting poverty through accessible and affordable credit solutions for education, clean energy, agribusiness, and water projects.

With our deep understanding of the African market, we've designed financial products that meet the unique needs of our customers, helping them achieve sustainable development goals while combating climate change.
