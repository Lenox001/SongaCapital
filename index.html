About Songa Capital Ltd

Songa Capital Ltd is a leading financial services provider in Kenya, dedicated to offering accessible and affordable loan solutions to individuals and small businesses.

With our deep understanding of the Kenyan market, we've designed loan products that meet the unique needs of our customers, helping them achieve their financial goals.

Secure & Licensed

Fully regulated by CBK with secure transactions and data protection.

Quick Processing
